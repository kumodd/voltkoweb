Our Mission

Voltko is a mission-driven EV charging platform built for the next billion electric kilometers. Born out of a passion for clean mobility and energy equity, we’re here to empower individuals, fleet operators, gig workers, and charging station owners with seamless access to reliable, affordable 2-wheeler and 3-wheeler electric charging infrastructure.

We’re not just building tech. We’re building trust, convenience, and a cleaner tomorrow.

Our Vision for the Future

We believe the future of urban transport is electric, decentralized, and inclusive. But adoption won’t happen unless charging becomes easy, accessible, and smart. That’s why Voltko is building a connected platform that bridges the gap between EV users and a growing ecosystem of charging stations.

Whether you’re a daily commuter, a delivery partner, or a fleet manager, Voltko gives you the power to locate, book, and pay for charging – all in one place.

The Principles That Guide Us

Sustainability

Our core purpose is to create a positive environmental impact by accelerating the shift to green mobility.

Inclusivity

We build solutions for everyone, ensuring our platform is affordable and easy to use for all Indians.

Innovation

We leverage technology to solve real-world problems, making charging smarter and more efficient.

Trust

We are committed to building a reliable network and a transparent platform our users can depend on.
