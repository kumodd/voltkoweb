The Gig Economy's Charging Crisis

High-utilization vehicles face a broken charging experience, leading to lost income and operational chaos.

Unreliable & Inefficient

Public stations are often occupied, out of order, or too slow, causing long queues and downtime for riders.

Poor Accessibility

No Smart Features

Multiple Revenue Streams for Scalable Growth

Our flexible model captures value from individual users, large fleets, and property partners.
